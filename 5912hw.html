Prior to starting the game, there are no pickups displayed. They are spawned in when the game starts. The first playable object is this blue ball.

Once the game starts, the pickups are spawned in. They each have their own unique particle FX.

Making contact with a pickup will transform the player into that object, deactivate the pickup and slightly change navigation behavior based on the new object's mesh.

The player can transform into a barrel. The barrel mesh forces the player to roll as if they were a barrel opposed to a sphere or box. Whenever the player moves, a trail follows behind.

Same logic applies with the cone pickup. Whenever a pickup is deactivated, the simulation checks every 10 seconds to spawn in a random collectible at a random location.
